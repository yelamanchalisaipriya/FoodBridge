About FoodBridge

FoodBridge is a platform that connects food donors, volunteers, and receivers to ensure that excess food reaches people who need it the most.

How It Works

1. Donate Food

Provide details about the available food.

2. Verification

Food details are verified before sharing.

3. Volunteer Pickup

Volunteers collect and deliver the food safely.

4. Successful Delivery

Food reaches NGOs and needy people on time.
