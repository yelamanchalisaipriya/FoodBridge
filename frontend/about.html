About FoodBridge

FoodBridge is a platform designed to connect food donors, volunteers, and organizations that help people in need. Our goal is to reduce food waste and ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it safely and quickly.

Our Mission

Millions of people go hungry every day while tons of food are wasted. Our mission is to bridge this gap by creating a simple and reliable food donation system.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no food goes to waste and no person sleeps hungry. Through technology and community support, FoodBridge aims to make food sharing easy and accessible.

How FoodBridge Works

1. Food Donation

Donors provide details of surplus food available.

2. Verification

Food information is verified for quality and safety.

3. Volunteer Support

Volunteers help collect and deliver the donated food.

4. Successful Delivery

Food reaches NGOs, shelters, and people in need.

Why Choose FoodBridge?

  • Reduce food waste effectively.
  • Support needy communities.
  • Fast and secure food donation process.
  • Connect donors, volunteers, and receivers.
  • Create a positive social impact.
